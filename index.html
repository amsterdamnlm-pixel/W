Who We Are

New Life Ministry is a Pentecostal community in Amsterdam dedicated to authentic worship, genuine connection, and meaningful service. We believe in the transformative power of faith and the importance of building a space where people can grow spiritually and belong fully.

Our mission is simple: reach people for Christ, build authentic community, and equip believers to live out their faith with purpose and clarity.
